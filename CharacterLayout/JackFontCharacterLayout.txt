Each character in JackFontCharacterLayout.png occupies a rectangle 8 pixels wide and 11 pixels high.

* The ascender is 9 pixels high (white background)
* The descender is 1 pixel high (red background)
* The line gap is 1 pixel high (blue)
* Letter spacing is 2 pixels wide (green)
* Word spacing is 8 pixels wide (the full width of a single character)
